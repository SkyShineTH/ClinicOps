## Design Context

### Users
Prospective dental patients in Bangkok use the marketing site to evaluate trust, service fit, clinic technology, locations, and to submit a demo booking request. Staff-facing demo users use `/app/*` to scan operational dashboards and understand front-desk workflows quickly.

### Brand Personality
Clinical Futures: premium, precise, calm, and future-ready. The interface should feel like a high-end dental clinic with advanced digital treatment planning, not a generic SaaS dashboard or loud sci-fi interface.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a bright porcelain clinical base with spectral teal, opal blue, muted gold, and restrained graphite. Prefer real clinic/healthcare stock photography with free-use licensing and visible source credits. Motion should feel like scanning, calibration, and reveal choreography: smooth, purposeful, and respectful of reduced-motion settings.

### Design Principles
- Lead with trust first, then innovation: futuristic detail must support confidence in care.
- Use real free-license photography for patient-facing surfaces; keep credits and source URLs available.
- Make the booking path feel guided and precise, with clear progress and responsive feedback.
- Staff screens should feel like a quiet command center: dense, legible, and calm under repeated use.
- Avoid decorative excess; use grids, scan lines, and subtle motion fields instead of blobs or generic glowing effects.
